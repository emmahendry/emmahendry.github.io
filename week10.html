Lab Tasks

Task 1: Optimise your video for Web use

After correlating my portfolio of video content, I chose to edit my video in iMovie. Once familiarizing myself with the operating system of the application I started to add my media into the project in my desired arrangement.

Once attaining my preferred timeline of clips, I then shortened down the length of some clips which I believed to be too extensive. To experiment further with the layout of my video I decided to add title text to the opening clip. When watching the draft, it seemed the colouring of the individual clips did not flow in a way which was aesthetically pleasing to view. To combat this, I edited the entire video with filters to create a cohesive theme.

I decided to add background music throughout the video. This proved difficult when editing clips which already had audio. To combat this, I split the background music when it came to clips with audio and lowered the volume from 20% (on all other clips) to 7%. This was to remain cohesive but not take away from the information presented in the audio.

When exporting the video following through with the tasks in step 6 I exported it in the 3 required video quality’s:

  1. High (1080p)
  2. Medium (720p)
  3. Low (480p)
